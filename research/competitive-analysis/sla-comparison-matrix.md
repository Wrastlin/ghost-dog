## Key Takeaways for ReliableClaim Positioning

### Where the bar is HIGH (match or exceed):
- Emergency on-site: 2-4 hours is table stakes
- Non-emergency contact: 24 hours maximum
- Warranty: 3-5 years is expected
- IICRC certifications: WRT minimum, AMRT for mold
- $1M GL / WC / Auto insurance minimums
- Xactimate proficiency

### Where the bar is LOW (easy to differentiate):
- Estimate upload speed: Most TPAs take 5-7 days; 24-48 hours stands out
- Transparency: Most TPAs don't publish their SLAs; publishing yours = trust signal
- Payment terms: Industry is 30-90 days; faster payment = contractor loyalty
- Onboarding speed: Most take months; Accuserve proved 1-day is possible
- Communication frequency: Few TPAs mandate specific update cadences
- Technology: Many still use fragmented systems; unified platform = differentiator

### Where nobody is competing yet:
- Real-time moisture analytics (only CoreLogic Mitigate and Encircle are here)
- AI-powered estimate review (Snapsheet and SettleAssist are early)
- Contractor wellness / sustainable workload management
- Public SLA dashboards (nobody does this today)
