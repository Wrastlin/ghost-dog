# Insurance Carrier-Specific Requirements

> Most carrier-specific SLA metrics are held behind login-protected portals.
> Below is what's publicly documented.

---

## State Farm - Select Service / Premier Service Program (PSP)

- Requires contractors to order materials from **Lowe's or Home Depot**
- Managed repair program with preferred vendor list
- Specific SLA metrics not publicly available (behind B2B portal)
- Source: ContractorTalk forum discussion

---

## Allstate - Good Hands Repair Network

- Managed repair contractor network
- Notable legal case: Allstate paid **$335,000** to settle dispute over $33,000 restoration invoice
  - Had falsely claimed "does not cover OH&P for mitigation or contents"
  - Source: https://www.candrmagazine.com/allstate-pays-contractor-335000-to-settle-dispute-over-33000-restoration-invoice/

---

## USAA
- Uses Contractor Connection network
- **3-year workmanship warranty** (vs. standard 5-year)
- Contractor Connection handles assignment and QA

---

## Nationwide
- Uses Alacrity Solutions (via ala-nw.com)
- **5-year workmanship warranty**
- Managed via AlacNet platform

---

## Farmers Insurance
- **Guaranteed Repair Program**
- BluSky is an approved vendor
- No publicly documented "Farmers Fast" program
- Uses Circle of Dependability / DRP (Direct Repair Program)

---

## Citizens Property Insurance (Florida)
- Uses **Contractor Connection** for managed repair
- Explicit SLA: contractors failing to maintain requirements are **removed from network**
- Published brochure and contractor agreements available (see Resource Index)
- Subject to Florida OIR market conduct examinations

## Xactimate / XactAnalysis (Verisk) - Platform Requirements

**Not carrier-specific SLAs**, but the platform through which SLAs are monitored:
- XactAnalysis SP gives contractors visibility into assignment details
- Quality Review automates reinspection workflows
- Estimate accuracy benchmarked against carrier guidelines
- Verisk does not publish universal SLAs -- each carrier sets their own within the platform
