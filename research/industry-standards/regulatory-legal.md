## Key Lawsuits

### Allstate v. Contractor (AOB Bad Faith - California)
- Allstate paid **$335,000** to settle dispute over $33,000 unpaid invoice
- Had falsely claimed "does not cover OH&P for mitigation or contents"
- Sources:
  - https://www.candrmagazine.com/allstate-pays-contractor-335000-to-settle-dispute-over-33000-restoration-invoice/
  - https://edcross.com/2023/06/21/allstate-pays-contractor-335000-to-settle-dispute-over-33000-restoration-invoice-cr/

### People's Trust Managed Repair Lawsuits (Florida)
- Multiple lawsuits alleging faulty workmanship, corner-cutting, deceptive practices
- In-house contractor Rapid Response Team
- *Bailey v. People Trust Insurance Company* (2024)
- Sources:
  - https://caselaw.findlaw.com/court/fl-district-court-of-appeal/116289665.html
  - https://www.insurancejournal.com/news/southeast/2024/10/17/797507.htm
  - https://www.insurancebusinessmag.com/us/news/legal-insights/peoples-trust-wins-florida-appeal-in-managed-repair-dispute-with-homeowners-535304.aspx

### FAPIA/NAPIA v. Velocity Risk Underwriters (Oct 2025)
- Anti-competitive endorsements barring policyholders from hiring public adjusters
- Sources:
  - https://www.insurancejournal.com/news/southeast/2025/10/14/843505.htm
  - Full complaint (PDF): https://www.insurancejournal.com/app/uploads/2025/10/FAPIA-complaint.pdf
