Three Big Differentiators for ReliableClaim:

1. Publish Your SLAs. No TPA does this publicly. It signals confidence and builds trust with carriers and contractors alike.
2. Pay Contractors Fast. Net 15 days vs. industry norm of 60-180 days. Attracts the best contractors who currently avoid TPA work.
3. Own the Communication Gap. 48-hour update cadence, 2-hour callbacks, milestone notifications. #1 unmet need in the industry.

TPA Profiles

Contractor Connection (Crawford)

Largest independently-managed network. 6,000+ contractors, $3B/yr, 500K assignments. 7 of top 10 US carriers.

  • POMS scoring: TIP (cycle time), CSAT, professionalism, estimate accuracy, quality
  • 5-point QA: Electronic audit → Scope audit (100% line items) → Re-inspection → CSAT monitoring → Performance monitoring
  • J.D. Power: Certified 5 consecutive years (2017-2021)
  • Golden Hammer Award: Top 100 contractors recognized annually
  • Removal: Must maintain "satisfactory rating" or face reduced assignments / removal
Hancock Claims (#1 RIA Credentialing)
  • Inspection scheduling: 4-8 hours
  • Deploy to any area: 24-48 hours
  • Report to adjuster: 24 hours
  • Cycle time: 7+ days faster than traditional
  • LAE reduction: 50%
  • CSAT improvement: 10-15% over baseline
  • Exemplar platform with required fields/checkpoints
  • Haag certification required for technicians
  • Payment via escrow until job approval
Accuserve
  • No signup/membership fees
  • 1-day onboarding via Sublynk partnership
  • IICRC WRT required on every water visit
  • IICRC AMRT required for mold
  • $1M GL / $2M agg, pollution liability required
  • Performance-based: top performers get more work
  • Quarterly Premier bonus: $1,000 for top performers
  • 84% resolution rate
Sedgwick
  • 2,000+ contractor locations
  • Iron Clad Warranty: 5 yr (replace), 3 yr (GC), 1 yr (repair)
  • If contractor fails, Sedgwick assumes warranty
  • Annual recertification required
  • File audits, re-inspections, NPS surveys
  • Monthly/quarterly/annual performance reports
  • Non-performers removed from network
Alacrity Solutions
  • Selling MRP division (Jan 2026) = market opportunity
  • 24/7/365 FNOL intake operations center
  • AlacNet platform for timelines, assignments, payment
  • 5-year workmanship warranty
  • Nationwide Insurance partner (ala-nw.com)
  • CGL + CPL + Pollution Liability required
  • Annual review: CSAT surveys, on-site visits, credentialing
National Networks (DKI, Paul Davis, BELFOR, PuroClean)

DKI: On-site within 2 hours. Min $500K revenue. Full-service required.

Paul Davis: IICRC-approved training center with flood house. QA dept for estimate review. Pioneered 3-day drying.

BELFOR: #1 Contractor on Top 500 Remodelers since 2001. Contributed to IICRC S500/S520 standards.

PuroClean: SPAR tiers (Strategic/Preferred/Approved/Restricted). SOC2 Type 2 for preferred. $80M national accounts in 2025. 500+ locations.

Legal / Regulatory Landscape

Key Lawsuits

Allstate $335K Settlement: Paid $335K to settle $33K restoration invoice. Falsely claimed OH&P not covered for mitigation.

People's Trust (FL): Multiple lawsuits for faulty workmanship, corner-cutting via in-house Rapid Response Team.

FAPIA vs. Velocity Risk (2025): Anti-competitive endorsements barring policyholders from hiring public adjusters.

Assignment of Benefits (AOB)

Florida: 405 AOB lawsuits (2006) → 28,200 (2016). HB 7065 signed 2019.

Texas: AOBs illegal and unenforceable. Contractors using limited POA workaround risk criminal penalties.

Unfair Claims Practice Act

Merlin Law Group argues depressed costs under preferred vendor programs don't restore insureds to pre-loss condition, potentially violating UCPA.
