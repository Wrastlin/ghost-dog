# Contractor Connection (Crawford & Company)

> **The largest independently-managed contractor network** in insurance restoration.

## Company Overview
- **Parent**: Crawford & Company (NYSE: CRD-A/CRD-B)
- **HQ**: Jacksonville, FL
- **Network**: 6,000+ rigorously vetted contractors globally
- **Volume**: ~500,000 assignments/year, ~$3B annual project cost
- **Markets**: US, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany
- **Clients**: 7 of top 10 US insurance providers
- **Contact**: 1 (800) 690-0174

## POMS Scoring System (Performance Objective Monitoring System)

**Score Components:**
1. **TIP (Time in Process)** -- Estimate upload speed / cycle time
2. **Customer Service Scores** -- Direct policyholder feedback
3. **Professionalism in Communication** -- Cooperation metrics
4. **Estimate Accuracy** -- 100% line-item review
5. **Overall Quality of Service** -- Workmanship and craftsmanship

- Monthly electronic scorecard delivered to each contractor
- Top 100 contractors awarded **Golden Hammer Award** annually at RESTORE Conference
- Higher tier: **"Contractor of the Year"** by region

---

## Five-Point Quality Assurance Program

1. **Electronic Audit** -- Automated compliance check on estimating system/pricing database
2. **Technical Scope & Quantity Audit** -- 100% of line items reviewed for accuracy
3. **Re-Inspection Program** -- Random re-inspections of completed work
4. **Customer Satisfaction Monitoring** -- Timeliness, professionalism, satisfaction surveys
5. **Contractor Performance Monitoring** -- Must maintain satisfactory rating or face removal

## Contractor Credentialing Requirements

- Insurance restoration experience required
- **$1M General Liability** minimum
- **$1M Automobile** insurance minimum
- **Workers' Compensation** coverage
- All required state licensing (current)
- Background checks on all job-site employees
- **12 quality references**
- Professional commercial/industrial zoned facilities
- **Xactimate** estimating software required
- Internet access and digital cameras
- Physical inspection of facilities and equipment
- Annual credentialing renewal
- **5-year workmanship warranty** (US standard; varies by carrier)

**Fee Model**: "Pay as you grow" -- application fee, then fees only for work performed.

## Penalties & Consequences

- Must maintain **"satisfactory performance rating"** to remain in network
- Poor performers receive reduced assignment volume
- Citizens Florida explicitly states: contractors failing requirements are **removed from network**
- Crawford employs **Network Performance Specialists** for enforcement
- Employee reviews note intense pressure to meet SLAs
