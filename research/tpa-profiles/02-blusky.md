# BluSky Restoration Contracting

> National restoration contractor operating as an insurance program vendor (not a TPA network manager like Contractor Connection).

## Company Overview
- **Website**: goblusky.com
- **Service model**: Direct restoration contractor (not a TPA that manages other contractors)
- **Coverage**: 40+ states at any given time
- **Philosophy**: "96 Proof" -- 96% customer re-hire rate
- **Insurance programs**: Approved vendor in Farmers Insurance Guaranteed Repair Program and others
- **Federal**: GSA contract holder (47QRAA24D00EL)

---

## Service Standards

- **24/7/365** emergency response with "instant deployment"
- First 24 hours post-loss emphasized as critical window
- Single project manager/superintendent per project end-to-end
- Detailed and timely reports including photographs and work logs
- VP of Managed Repair Programs leads insurance program relationships

---

## Trade Partner / Subcontractor Requirements

BluSky uses a **Trade Partner Qualification Packet** for subcontractors:
- Master Subcontractor Agreement
- Subcontractor Work Order
- W-9
- Work & Services / Licenses documentation
- Certification of Business Designation
- Company Info & References
- Insurance Documents (proof of relevant licenses and insurance)
- Workers Comp Exempt Status Form
- Jobsite Rules acknowledgment
- IRCA compliance
- OFAC, Anti-Bribery, and Fraud compliance forms
- Must comply with all occupational health, safety, and accident prevention laws
