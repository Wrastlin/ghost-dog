# Alacrity Solutions

> Major TPA providing managed repair, claims management, and contractor network services.
> **Note**: Alacrity announced the **strategic sale of its Managed Repair Division** on January 20, 2026.

## Company Overview
- **Website**: alacritysolutions.com
- **Operations**: 24/7/365 operations center for FNOL intake
- **Key system**: AlacNet -- manages timelines, contractor assignments, payment
- **Notable client**: Nationwide Insurance (via ala-nw.com), Tower Hill/CastleCare

---

## SLA & Contractor Requirements

- Annual contractor review: customer satisfaction surveys, on-site visits, credentialing
- Regional Field Managers (RFMs) conduct job site visits for compliance
- Customer Advocates measured on communication structure and program requirements
- Bi-annual continuing education for Customer Advocates
- **Certificate of Satisfaction** issued with workmanship and materials warranty
- **5-year workmanship warranty** standard

### Contractor Screening
- Criminal background checks
- Current licenses and certifications
- Financial stability demonstration
- Proof of insurance

---

## Insurance Requirements (from official PDF)

- CGL and CPL with Contractual Liability coverage
- No eroding/burning/defense-within-limits policies allowed
- Waiver of Subrogation in favor of Network Administrator
- Pollution Liability retroactive to contract date with 3-year extended discovery clause
- ISO form CA 00 01 10 13 for Auto coverage
- Declarations Page and Schedule of Forms must be attached to COI
