# Sedgwick - Managed Repair Program

> Major TPA with 2,000+ contractor locations across all major and secondary markets.

## Company Overview
- **Website**: sedgwick.com/repair-solutions/
- **Network**: 2,000+ locations
- **Legacy**: Acquired VeriClaim and First Choice Repair

---

## Contractor SLA Requirements

- All providers undergo thorough credentialing and **annual recertification**
- Built-in performance rating program monitoring all service standards
- Contractors receive frequent ratings from first claim
- **File Cycle Time Standards** tracked as a rating metric
- Monthly, quarterly, and annual performance reports for clients
- **Contractors who do not perform within standards are removed**

---

## Quality Assurance Program

- **File Audits**: Desk audits for technical accuracy of estimates and documentation
- **Re-inspections**: Physical re-inspections measuring satisfaction, technical accuracy, quality
- **Customer Service Surveys**: Timeliness, professionalism, quality, Net Promoter Score
- **Performance Metrics**: Captured, measured, shared with each contractor

---

## Iron Clad Warranty

| Work Type | Warranty Period |
|-----------|----------------|
| Roofs, siding, gutter **replacements** | 5 years |
| Roofs, siding, gutter **repairs** | 1 year |
| General contracting (replacements and repairs) | 3 years |

**Key feature**: If a contractor goes out of business, Sedgwick assumes the remainder of the warranty.
