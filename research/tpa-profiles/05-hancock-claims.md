## Contractor Standards

- Haag-certified technicians required
- Background in roofing, construction, or claims adjusting
- Valid driver's license + truck capable of transporting 32-foot ladder
- All vendors vetted via **ProfileGorilla** for licensure, insurance, performance
- "Best in Class" assignment based on location, capacity, carrier-specific metrics
- Top performers receive more work via algorithmic assignment
- All issues tracked and fed into assignment algorithm
- Payment via escrow: funds held until job completion and approval
