# Accuserve

> Performance-based contractor network with streamlined onboarding via Sublynk partnership.

## Company Overview
- **Website**: accuserve.com
- **Model**: Performance-based claim assignment (top performers get more work)
- **Resolution rate**: 84%
- **Fees**: No signup, membership, or renewal fees
- **Onboarding**: Reduced from months to one day (Sublynk partnership, Oct 2024)
- **Recognition**: Quarterly Premier Program ($1,000 bonus for top performers)

---

## Insurance Requirements

| Coverage | Requirement |
|----------|------------|
| General Liability | $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate |
| Workers' Comp | Per state statutory minimum (waived if no W-2 employees w/ attestation) |
| Auto/HNOA | Hired and Non-Owned Auto endorsement required |
| Pollution Liability (Water Mitigation) | Must define pollutants to include mold, microbial matter, fungus, biological substances; must not exclude asbestos/lead; no insured-vs-insured exclusion |
| Additional Insured | CG 2010 07 04 and CG 2037 07 04 required |
| Waiver of Subrogation | In favor of Accuserve Solutions, Inc. |
| Subcontractor work | Must not be excluded |

## Certification Requirements

| Certification | Status |
|--------------|--------|
| IICRC WRT (Water Restoration Technician) | **Required** on-site for every water mitigation visit |
| ASD certification | Preferred |
| IICRC AMRT | **Required** for mold remediation |
| State/local certifications | As mandated |
