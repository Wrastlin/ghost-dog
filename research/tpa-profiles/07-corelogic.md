---

## SLA & KPI Framework

- Each performance measure defined in SLA contract between carrier and restoration company
- Time component tethered to most service requirements
- Insurers use KPIs to rate vendors and award work
- **VPASS (Value Progression Adjusted Sigma Score)**: Real-time compliance monitoring based on Six Sigma
- Restoration companies must complete each service requirement within predetermined time periods

---

## Quality Standards

- IICRC Water Restoration Technician certification required at minimum
- Business stability and financial records evaluated
- Customer satisfaction scores tracked
- SOC 2 Type II certified (SettleAssist)
